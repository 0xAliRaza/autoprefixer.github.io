What is it

Autoprefixer is a service for managing vendor prefixes. It adds missing prefixes and deletes obsolete ones. It is recommended by Google and used by Twitter and Taobao.

{% include ads.html %}

How does it work

The autoprefixer uses data on the popularity of browsers and support for vendor prefixes by browsers. Based on this information, it arranges and deletes the prefixes.

Browser filter

You can configure which browsers to generate prefixes for in the distribution. More details on the syntax are in the documentation. There is also a block in the FAQ.
